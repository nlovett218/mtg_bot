Magic: King of the Discord

This is a simple text-based version of the popular card game Magic the Gathering. This bot provides a simple and fun experience for the casual player, whether you have or haven't played Magic the Gathering before. The premise is simple, collect as many cards as you can and use them to your advantage to control the battlefield, whether they be creature cards, enchantment cards or instant/sorcery cards. 

Think of it as a Discord MOBA.

Creatures and Spells

Each draw will be something new. You never know what you will get. It may help you, or it may hinder you to increase the thrill of the game; to be king of the discord. You define the battlefield. There are currently over 120 unique cards with the possibility of having a special attribute or ability. Most of these are made up cards, but you might see some that you recognize.

Rewarding to Play

You are rewarded for each action that you take, opening packs, attacking, using claims, voting, etc.

Opening Packs

Packs

Packs keep your deck from reaching 0. You earn them buy claiming, and purchasing them with Magic Beans, the currency for the bot. You get 2 lands, 2 commons, 2 uncommons, and 1 rare or mythic for each pack opened.
